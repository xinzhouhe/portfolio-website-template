# About Me

![Daniel at Yellowstone](https://xinzhouhe.github.io/src/imgs/me.JPG)

Hello! I am Daniel X. He, I am a first-year M.S. student at the [Department of Computer Science and Engineering](https://www.cse.osu.edu/) at The Ohio State University. I earned my B.S.E. degree in Computer Science from the [University of Michigan College of Engineering](https://www.engin.umich.edu/) in December 2024.


My current research focus is on STEM education, to explore methods that not only engage students more effectively, but also help them understand the underlying concepts rather than merely memorizing facts.


Beyond STEM education, I am also interested in operating systems, computer networks, and distributed systems, with a particular focus on making these systems more efficient, reliable, and fault-tolerant.
